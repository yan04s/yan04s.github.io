Data Deletion Request

We respect your right to privacy. If you would like us to delete any personal information we have collected from you through this website, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Erasure

Send an email to: support@nacc.uno

Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

Include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used on our site
  • Reason for the request (optional)

What Happens Next?

After verifying your identity, we will delete your personal data from our records within 30 days, unless retention is legally required (e.g., tax or order records).

A confirmation email will be sent once the request is completed.
